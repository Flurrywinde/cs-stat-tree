Choicescript Analyzer

Convert your choicescript scene into a tree of choice/if branches, condensed down to show only where a stat can have more than one possible value. Hover over the nodes with thick red outlines to see what the stats can be at that part of the story. Hover over condensed nodes to see what's inside. Note: Not all choicescript features are supported yet, e.g. *elif and *else, etc.
